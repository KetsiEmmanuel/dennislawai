AI Policy for Dennis Law

Introduction

At Dennis Law, we are committed to ensuring that our AI technologies are used responsibly and ethically. This policy outlines our approach to AI and its implications for our clients and stakeholders.

Data Privacy

We respect the privacy of our clients and are committed to protecting personal information. Our AI systems are designed to comply with applicable data protection laws and regulations.

Accuracy and Reliability

We strive to ensure that our AI systems provide accurate and reliable information. However, users are encouraged to validate AI-generated information and consult with legal professionals for critical decisions.

Transparency

We believe in transparency regarding how our AI systems operate. Clients have the right to understand how their data is used and how AI-generated outcomes are derived.

Accountability

We take accountability seriously. Our team regularly reviews AI outputs to ensure compliance with our ethical standards and to address any potential biases or errors.

Continuous Improvement

We are committed to continuously improving our AI systems through regular updates, training, and stakeholder feedback.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our AI Policy, please contact us at info@dennislaw.com.
